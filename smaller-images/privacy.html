Privacy Policy — Smaller Image

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Smaller Image ("the App") is developed by MikiShine. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, uses, and shares.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • All image processing is performed locally on your Mac. Your images are never uploaded to any server.
  • The App does not use analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services.
  • The App does not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or browsing history.

Local Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • User preferences (compression settings, language choice, presets) via macOS UserDefaults.
  • Usage counter (daily compression count for free-tier enforcement) stored locally in encrypted form.
  • Purchase status managed by Apple's StoreKit framework.

This data never leaves your device.

In-App Purchases

Subscription and purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
