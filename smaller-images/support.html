FAQ

How many images can I compress for free?

You can compress up to 10 images per day for free. Upgrade to Smaller Pro for unlimited daily compressions.

What formats are supported?

Smaller Image supports PNG, JPEG, HEIC, WebP, TIFF, and GIF as input. Output is PNG, JPEG, HEIC, WebP, or TIFF. (GIF input is converted to a single-frame output — animation is not preserved.)

Where are my compressed images saved?

Compressed images are saved to a temporary location. Use the Export button or drag files directly to any Finder folder to save them permanently.

Are my images uploaded to any server?

No. All processing happens locally on your Mac. Your images never leave your device.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open the app, click the usage badge in the top bar to open the upgrade screen, then click Restore Purchases at the bottom.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open System Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Smaller Pro → Cancel.
